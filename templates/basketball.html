    Weighted Anti-Grip Gloves Shop

  • Product Features

    POWERHANDZ Anti-Grip Weighted Training Gloves have weight distributed across the top side of the gloves and an anti-friction material on the palm side of the gloves, making it more difficult to dribble a basketball when training.

    • Weight resistance on the gloves improves strength in the hand and arm muscles
    • Increased hand speed
    • Makes dribbling and catching the ball more difficult during training
    • Improved confidence once gloves are removed
    • Up to 2 LB in weight for each pair based on size

    Basketball POWERARCH Shop

  • Product Features

    The POWERARCH is a first of its kind training accessory designed to force a ball handler on the basketball court to stay low with their dribble. Previously, athletes were forced to use chairs or other items not designed for ball handling to work on staying low with their dribble.

    • Weight resistance on the gloves improves strength in the hand and arm muscles
    • Increased hand speed
    • Makes dribbling and catching the ball more difficult during training
    • Improved confidence once gloves are removed
  • Product Sizing

    The POWERARCH is 13" high and 22" wide.

    • 3-foot-5 standing barrier created to mimic a real defender
