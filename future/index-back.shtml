Peyton Kemp

Peyton Kemp, is an 8 year old 3rd grader from Michigan. He has been playing basketball since he was 4 years old he started training when he was 6 years old. He enjoys school, playing video games, dancing, singing, and his family. Peyton, is an all A student athlete he excels in the classroom as well as on the court. He has been playing travel basketball for 1 year now this season will be his 2nd year.  Peyton, is considered one of the top players in the class of 2026. He trains daily to improve his skill set and to become a better player overall. Peyton, doesn't want to be a good basketball player he's wants to be a great one! Peyton's, goal is to attend college on an academic or athletic scholarship and possibly a professional basketball player. If that doesn't workout he wants to become a doctor.

Collin Tjin


My name is Collin Tjin and I’m a eight year old from Southern California! I’ve started playing sports just after turning three but it wasn’t until I turned four that I found my love for basketball. It all started out trying to dribble a couple of times in a row without messing up and I haven’t looked back since. Besides family, school and my faith, basketball is a huge part of my day to day activities from the time I wake up to when I go to bed. If I’m awake, there’s a good chance I have a basketball in my hand and if I’m not dribbling, I’m watching basketball videos on YouTube, watching games on TV or out in the driveway playing. Besides my love for basketball, the reason why I play is because of all of the great people I that I’ve had the chance to meet such as teammates and coaches, as well as the amazing opportunities I’ve had such as playing on the Staples Center floor after watching my favorite team, the Los Angeles Clippers!
